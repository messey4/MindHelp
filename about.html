ABOUT US

MindHelp is a Nigerian-based non-governmental organization that saves and improves adults round the world. We provide hope, support, and resources to individuals struggling with depression, and to promote greater understanding and awareness of mental health issues in our community.

WHAT IS DEPRESSION?

Depression (major depressive disorder) is a common and serious medical illness that negatively affects how you feel, the way you think and how you act.

WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

Untreated depression can have serious consequences, including increased risk of suicide, substance abuse, and other mental health disorders.

WHAT DOES MINDHELP OFFER?

  • Diagnosis: We take diagnosis based on: Physical exam, Lab tests, Psychiatric evaluation, DSM-5.
  • Treatment: We help relieve depressive patients with psychotherapy and medications.

FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE?

We support patients who we have diagnosed with depression, financially, based on our eligibility criteria. Assistance with: money to meet minimum requirements, job training and support, and financial counseling.

WHO DO WE SUPPORT?

MINDHELP supports vulnerable adults, anyone who is 30 years of age or older and who has mental inability to care for himself or herself.

Bringing hope and healing to those in need.

ADVOCACY CAMPAIGNS AND OUTREACHES

MindFirst engages in advocacy efforts to raise awareness about the positive effects of exercise on mental health, specifically depression.

HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE

MindFirst provides a range of services to help individuals and communities affected by depression which includes: Mental health services; Access to medications; Support groups and Mental Health Training. We help individuals and communities access the care and support they need to recover and thrive.
