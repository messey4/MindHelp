TIPS TO MANAGE DEPRESSION

Depression can make you feel like nothing will help, or that any relief will be temporary, and it can create a cycle of maladaptive thinking, feeling, and doing (or non-doing). However, depression is treatable, and in addition to psychiatric treatment and therapy.

HERE ARE SIX TIPS FROM OUR MINDHELP EXPERTS:

GET ACTIVE

30 minutes of brisk physical activity daily is ideal!

NOURISH YOUR BODY

Eat well-balanced meals and avoid high-sugar foods.

SLEEP

Getting enough sleep is good for your mind and body.

SET REALISTIC GOALS

Break your goals down and celebrate your little successes!

REDUCE PROCASTINATION

Do things a little at a time rather than avoid tasks all together.

PRACTICE SELF COMPASSION

Talk to yourself, like you would a loved one!
